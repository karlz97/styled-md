# Styled-md Create beautiful portable & pritable files from markdown!
## Introduction *Styled-md* is an opensource app that creates beautiful portable & pritable files (.pdf, .png and .webarchive) from markdown! - **Easy to use**: Just write your markdown and see the result in real-time. - **Template-market**: Build your document from a variety of templates: resume, letter, invitation, and more. - **Save your doc**: Save it as .png .webarchive or print as pdf! - **Local, light and private**: This app runs completely on your browser, no data is sent to the server. - **Open-source** ## About the App **Buy Me a Coffee**: If this app helped you twice or more, please consider buying me a coffee to support my work.
